The Problem

Active people are forced to pause
the sport they love when they travel

Over a billion people participate in sports regularly. When they travel, they face a trio of friction points that no existing platform solves end-to-end.

🎒

No equipment abroad

Bikes, boards, rackets, and wetsuits are impractical to carry on planes. Rental shops are expensive, unreliable, or simply non-existent at the destination.

🗺️

No local knowledge

The best trails, courts, pools, and surf breaks are hidden from tourists. Finding safe, suitable spots — and the right difficulty level — is a major research burden.

👤

No trustworthy local partner

Many sports require a buddy for safety or simply work better socially. Finding a verified local who matches your level, language, and schedule is nearly impossible today.

"I travel six times a year for work and always have to skip my morning rides. I'd pay good money to cycle with a local who knows the roads." — Target user archetype

Why 15% works

Airbnb charges 3–14%; Fiverr 20%; Airbnb Experiences 20%. SharedXP's 15% is competitive for a high-trust, safety-critical activity marketplace where hosts bring significant value (equipment, local knowledge, liability coverage).

The XP loyalty program reinforces repeat behavior without discounting — protecting the margin while improving retention.

vs. Airbnb Experiences

SharedXP is sports-first and structured (verified levels, equipment, safety). Airbnb Experiences is generic and skewed toward tourist activities with professional guides, not peer-to-peer sport.

vs. Strava / Komoot

These are activity tracking and route discovery tools — no transactional layer, no host-guest model, no equipment sharing. SharedXP adds commerce on top of social sport discovery.

vs. Gear Rentals

Gear rental is a commodity with thin margins and logistics overhead. SharedXP bundles equipment access with local expertise, social experience, and a loyalty flywheel — far higher value per transaction.

Risks & Mitigations

Known challenges and
how we address them

⚠ Chicken-and-egg marketplace

Classic cold-start: guests won't come without hosts; hosts won't list without guests.

✔ Mitigation

Supply-first strategy: seed 20–50 hosts per city before demand launch. City champions guaranteed. First 3 bookings commission-free for hosts.

⚠ Safety & liability

Physical activities carry injury risk. A serious incident could damage trust platform-wide.

✔ Mitigation

Full legal suite live (Terms, Safety Policy, Disclaimers, Dispute Resolution). Profile verification, six-dimension reviews, dispute system, and insurance partner integration in Phase 2.

⚠ Airbnb / Strava entry

A large platform could replicate sports-specific features quickly.

✔ Mitigation

Community network effects (XP, The Field, reviews) are hard to copy. Pure-play focus allows deeper sport-specific features than any generalist. First-mover in C2C sports.

⚠ Payment regulation

Stripe Connect, KYC/AML, and local tax compliance across 177 countries is complex.

✔ Mitigation

Launch in EU/EEA first (lower regulatory complexity). Stripe handles KYC and splits. Expand jurisdictions gradually as compliance is confirmed.
